About AMMPAD

AMMPAD is a new cryptocurrency based on the blockchain's multi-chain launchpad, including Binance smart chain, Solana, polygon, Matic, Kucoin and Huabi Eco chain.

AMMPAD's Multi-Chain IDO LaunchPad enables developers to simultaneously launch and innovate their Crypto Projects or Tokens on various blockchains. Through AMMPAD Incubator, the company will act as a catalyst for innovative and rising blockchain firms, acting as a one-stop-shop for boosting the growth of new blockchain ventures.

Features

Launch Pad For Ammpad Multi - Chain Ido

It will be possible for developers to launch and innovate their Crypto Projects or Tokens across various blockchains using the Multi-Chain IDO Launch Pad provided by AMMPAD.

Ammpad Incubator

Aiming to serve as a catalyst for innovative and rising blockchain firms, the AMMPAD Incubator will function as a one-stop shop for speeding the development of new blockchain initiatives.

Mutichain Ammpad Amm Dex

AMMPAD DEX is a blockchain-based liquidity protocol that aggregates liquidity from a diverse variety of reserves, ensuring minimal slippage, the best exchange rates, and insurance for liquidity pool providers across the whole Defi ecosystem.

Launchpad

AMMPAD's launchpad will give investors a platform that will allow them to earn, participate in and benefit from transparent, secure, and guaranteed Initial decentralised Exchange offerings through the use of its allocation mechanism.

AMMPAD Dex

A blockchain-based liquidity protocol, developed by AMMPAD, will aggregate liquidity from a wide range of reserves, resulting in the lowest slippage, the best exchange rates, and insurance for liquidity pool providers throughout the whole Defi ecosystem.

Roadmap

The Timeline

Q4, 2021

  • Team development.
  • Website development.
  • White paper development.
  • Project research & development.

Q1, 2022

  • Conceptual creation of Platform for KCC, BSC & Heco Chain, and development on Ethereum Testnet.
  • Incubation by ExNetwork Capital.
  • Community development.
  • Presale and private funding.
  • Partnerships.
  • Code Audit.
  • Documentation.

Q2, 2022

  • Launchpad v1 with KCC, BSC, and Heco Chain.
  • Private and public funding.
  • AMMPAD Token Generation Event (TGE).
  • Token DEX & CEX Listings.
